Gypsum board, also known as drywall, plasterboard, or wallboard, is a versatile and cost-effective building material that is widely used for interior walls and ceilings in residential and commercial spaces. Gypsum board is made of gypsum, a mineral that is abundant and environmentally friendly, and paper, a renewable resource that can be recycled. Asiatic Gypsum board has many advantages

- It is easy to install and repair, requiring only simple tools and skills.
- It is fire-resistant, sound-insulating, moisture-resistant, and mold-resistant, providing safety and comfort for the occupants.
- It is smooth and flat, allowing for various finishes and decorations, such as paint, wallpaper, or tiles.
- It is lightweight and flexible, reducing the load on the structure and allowing for creative designs and shapes.
